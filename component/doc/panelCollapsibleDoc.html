panelCollapsible

The panelCollapsible component is comprised of two parts: the content area, where its children can be displayed; and a header section, which can be clicked on, to cause the content area to collapse into not being visible, or expand to become visible. The panelCollapsible's state of being expanded or collapsed is fully controllable via its expanded attribute, which can be tied to a bean property through a ValueBinding.

Example

<ice:panelCollapsible expanded="#{true}">
    <f:facet name="header">
        <ice:panelGroup>
            <ice:outputText value="User Profile"/>
        </ice:panelGroup>
    </f:facet>
    <ice:panelGroup>
        <ice:panelGrid columns="2">
            <ice:outputLabel for="formFirstName" value="First Name"/>
            <ice:inputText id="formFirstName" value="#{bean.firstName}"/>

            <ice:outputLabel for="formLastName" value="Last Name"/>
            <ice:inputText id="formLastName" value="#{bean.lastName}"/>

            <ice:outputLabel for="formPhone" value="Phone Number"/>
            <ice:inputText id="formPhone" value="#{bean.phone}"/>

            <ice:outputLabel for="formEmail" value="Email"/>
            <ice:inputText id="formEmail" value="#{bean.email}"/>
        </ice:panelGrid>
    </ice:panelGroup>
</ice:panelCollapsible>
